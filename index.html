Information

You'd like to take a walk on a sea promenade? But currently there’s no sea promenade nearby? Then try out Sea! With Sea you can build your own sea soundscape.

ABOUT

In a complicated and stressed world you need time to relax and get back to your full strength. One of the best things you can do then is to go out and take a walk and find a relaxed place. But sometimes there is not enough time to such things and all you can do is to just lay back in your chair, close your eyes and try to imagine a relaxing situation.

With Sea I created an application that allows you to create your own changeable sea soundscape, when you don‘t have the possibility to go out for a walk along the coast. You can choose the soundscape and regulate intensities of elements as you wish. You like more birds? Or a stormy water? Or a walk on a rainy sea promenade? Harbor and ship sounds? No problem. Your changes will act immediately. You can build your own coast or take a sound dive in a random setting. Sit back and enjoy. And if you prefer a walk in a forest you can check out my complementary project Forest to create your own forest soundscape.

INSTALLATION

Sea is a Progressive Web App. You can install it via your browser. Just open the menu of your browser. Depending on your browser (Chrome, Firefox etc.) you have the option to "install" or "add to home screen". When you select one of those two options Sea will be added to your homescreen. A so called "Service worker" should download all required files and save them in the browser's cache (this may take a while, so you should have a good internet connection). Warning: After it is finished you sould be able start the app even if you don't have an internet connection. The PWA is in a beta status, so it might be that it only works fine with an internet connection. I recommend to use the app online until the offline support is approved.
